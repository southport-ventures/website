Hi, we're Southport Ventures
We buy niche, profitable SaaS businesses

We're an acquisition fund based on a simple idea:
“SaaS is a durable, value-oriented business model.”

We’re focused on businesses that reflect our value-oriented peers in the traditional economy. Niche, highly profitable, mission critical products with deep domain connections. SaaS businesses have obvious operational advantages in distribution and geographic flexibility. We believe the greatest advantage is a tale as old as time: Businesses will pay for and retain essential tools that make the difference for their bottom line.

Why Sell to Us?

We’ll take care of your customers and employees, invest in growing the business, and provide an outstanding outcome for you. Win. Win. Win.

We're not a private equity firm with a drawn-out process that will waste months of your time. We'll be clear in our communication, quick in our due diligence, and work closely and quickly with you to determine whether a sale makes sense.
